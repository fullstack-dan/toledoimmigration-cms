U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) agents will soon be present in Charlotte, NC for immigration operations, which has prompted concern and questions within the community. This blog shares information to increase awareness and empower community members in both North and South Carolina and those served by the Law Office of Mariana Toledo-Hermina.

CBP Operations in Charlotte

Local officials have confirmed that CBP agents are arriving in Charlotte for federal immigration enforcement as early as this weekend and possibly into next week. While the exact details of their activities remain unclear, the Mecklenburg County Sheriff and other local authorities have clarified that they are not directly participating in these operations and that routine law enforcement will continue independently of immigration enforcement by federal officials. 

Community Impact and Support

News of enhanced federal presence often creates anxiety and confusion. Local leaders, including school and city officials, are encouraging residents to avoid rumors and to seek out accurate, official information to maintain calm and safety. Community members are urged to know their rights and stay informed about developments. 

Know Your Rights

The Law Office of Mariana Toledo-Hermina is committed to supporting all residents of the Carolinas during these uncertain times. Everyone has rights under the law, regardless of immigration status. Our website’s “Know Your Rights” materials offer guidance on what to do if you encounter immigration agents, how to protect your family, and how to seek legal assistance. We encourage everyone to review these resources and share them with friends and family to ensure everyone is prepared and informed. For more information or legal support, please visit our website or contact our office directly.
