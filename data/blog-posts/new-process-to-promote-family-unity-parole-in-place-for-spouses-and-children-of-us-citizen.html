On June 18th, President Biden announced a new process to help U.S. citizens with noncitizen spouses and children who have been here for 10 years or more keep their families together. This new action - which will help certain noncitizen spouses and children apply for lawful permanent residence without leaving the country is available as of August 19, 2024. As of now, eligible spouses and children of U.S. citizens are able to apply for this process to obtain legal status while remaining with their families.
